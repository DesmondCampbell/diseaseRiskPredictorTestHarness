# D Campbell 2.3.15

title
5. Non-Congenital Disease
  
description
This disease is not congenital, i.e not-necessarily manifest from birth.
The age of onset curve tells you what proportion of those destined to eventually be affected with the disease, manifest it by a given age.
The disease's age of onset curve is specified in an 'ageOfOnset' table.
The 'expressedProportionOfLifetimeRisk' column of the 'ageOfOnset' table holds the above mentioned proportion.
